About

FanFaction was born out of a deep passion for football and the desire to create a space where fans can freely share their thoughts, ideas, and support for their favorite clubs. Our goal is to build a thriving community where everyone has a voice. This project began as a small idea in 2023 when I wanted a platform to discuss the highs and lows of my favorite club.

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, developed with dedication and expertise. Learn more about Holberton School here.

Explore our GitHub repository for more details about the project.
